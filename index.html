Eggcellence 2: Hard Boiled

The objective of this game is to survive the longest with the highest score. To score points, you must shoot eggs at houses and people but every time you hit a person they get faster.

Instructions:
  • Move player with arrow keys.
  • To shoot eggs at tap the space bar.
  • The higher your score the fast the villagers chase you.
  • If you hit a villager they get faster
  • Your goal is to get the highest score. Good Luck!
